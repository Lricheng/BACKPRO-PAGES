Support

如果你在 Back Pro 中遇到使用问题，可以先查看下方常见问题。仍无法解决时，欢迎通过邮件联系我们。

If you run into issues with Back Pro, check the FAQs below first. If you still need help, contact us by email.

Back Pro 能做什么？

Back Pro 帮助你为手机背板/背贴创建设计：选择机型 → 在画布上添加图片、文字、Emoji 与画笔 → 实时预览效果 → 导出 PNG 或 PDF 用于打印。

常见问题

如何导出打印文件？

在编辑器完成设计后进入导出页，选择相纸尺寸（或使用「自定义相纸」），然后选择导出格式：PNG 会保存到系统「照片」；PDF 会通过 iOS 文件选择器让你选择保存位置。导出前可先预览效果。

设计历史存在哪里？如何删除？

所有设计历史保存在你的 iPhone 本地，不会上传到 Back Pro 服务器。你可以在首页进入「历史记录」查看、恢复或删除设计。删除自定义机型时，App 会提示并清理关联的历史记录与图片资产。

AI 贴纸提示「设备不支持」怎么办？

部分旧款或特定设备可能不支持设备端 AI 抠图。你仍可使用相册图片、文字图层、Emoji 贴纸与画笔继续完成设计，不影响其他编辑与导出功能。

免费 AI 次数用完了怎么办？

免费版包含 3 次成功的 AI 贴纸生成。取消选择、处理失败或未识别到主体不会扣减次数。次数用完后，你可以购买 Back Pro 终身版（com.richeng.backpro.lifetime）解锁不限次数 AI 贴纸，或继续使用图片、文字、Emoji 等非 AI 功能。

如何恢复购买？

打开 App → 设置 →「恢复购买」。请确保当前设备登录的 Apple ID 与购买时一致。Back Pro 终身版支持家庭共享，具体共享范围以 App Store 家庭共享规则为准。

如何切换 App 语言？

打开 App → 设置 →「语言」，可选择「跟随系统」「简体中文」或「English」。

自定义机型有哪些尺寸限制？

背板宽高需大于 0mm 且不超过 200×300mm；背板圆角 0–50mm 且不超过短边一半。机身尺寸会自动补齐，也可手动调整，需保证背板完整落在机身轮廓内。详细规则见 App 内新增机型表单提示。

导出的文件为什么有水印？

免费版导出的 PNG/PDF 会在底部居中显示 Back Pro 水印。购买并解锁 Back Pro 终身版后，导出文件不再包含水印。

免费版与终身版

  • 免费版：完整编辑、预览、历史记录与导出；含 3 次成功 AI 贴纸；导出带水印。
  • 终身版：不限次数 AI 贴纸、无水印导出，以及后续在同一 App 内发布的终身版功能。

What does Back Pro do?

Back Pro helps you create phone back plate and back sticker designs: choose a device model → add images, text, emoji, and drawing on canvas → preview in real time → export PNG or PDF for printing.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I export a print-ready file?

After finishing your design, open the export screen, choose a paper size (or use Custom Paper), then pick a format: PNG saves to the system Photos library; PDF uses the iOS file picker so you can choose where to save. You can preview before exporting.

Where is design history stored? How do I delete it?

All design history is stored locally on your iPhone and is not uploaded to Back Pro servers. Open History from the home screen to view, restore, or delete designs. Deleting a custom device model prompts you to remove associated history records and image assets as well.

What if AI stickers say my device is not supported?

Some older or specific devices may not support on-device AI cutout. You can still add photos, text layers, emoji stickers, and drawing to finish your design. Other editing and export features are unaffected.

What happens when my free AI uses run out?

The free version includes 3 successful AI sticker generations. Cancelled selections, failed processing, and no-subject results do not consume a free use. After that, you can purchase Back Pro Lifetime (com.richeng.backpro.lifetime) for unlimited AI stickers, or continue using non-AI features such as photos, text, and emoji.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the app → Settings → Restore Purchase. Make sure the Apple ID signed in on your device matches the one used to purchase. Back Pro Lifetime supports Family Sharing under App Store Family Sharing rules.

How do I change the app language?

Open the app → Settings → Language, then choose System Default, Simplified Chinese, or English.

What are the limits for custom device sizes?

Back plate width and height must be greater than 0 mm and no larger than 200×300 mm. Corner radius is 0–50 mm and cannot exceed half of the shorter side. Body geometry is auto-filled but can be adjusted; the back plate must remain fully inside the body outline. See in-app hints on the add-device form for details.

Why does my export have a watermark?

Free exports include a centered Back Pro watermark at the bottom of PNG/PDF files. After unlocking Back Pro Lifetime, exports are watermark-free.

Free vs Lifetime

  • Free: full editing, preview, history, and export; 3 successful AI stickers; exports include a watermark.
  • Lifetime: unlimited AI stickers, watermark-free export, and future Lifetime features within the same app.
